# From Data to Calculation

Once we understand the structure of outbreak data, we can begin calculating meaningful measures such as vaccine effectiveness.

## The Concept Behind the Calculation

Vaccine effectiveness compares how risk differs between vaccinated and unvaccinated groups.

## The Formula

VE = (1 − Relative Risk) × 100

## Working Through the Calculation

Reveal\
Step 1 :: Identify the number of cases and total population in each group\
Step 2 :: Calculate risk in each group\
Step 3 :: Divide risks to obtain relative risk\
Step 4 :: Convert to vaccine effectiveness

## Applying to Our Example

Callout :: note\
Text :: In this outbreak, the vaccinated group has much lower risk, leading to a high effectiveness estimate

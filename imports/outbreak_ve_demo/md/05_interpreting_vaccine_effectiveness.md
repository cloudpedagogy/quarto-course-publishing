# Moving from Calculation to Meaning

Calculating vaccine effectiveness is only the first step. Interpreting the result correctly is essential.

## Understanding the Result

Callout :: important\
Text :: A vaccine effectiveness of 80% indicates a reduction in risk, not complete protection

## Interpreting in Context

Tabs\
Meaning :: Indicates reduced probability of infection\
Assumptions :: Groups must be comparable\
Limitations :: External factors may influence results

## Avoiding Common Errors

Reveal\
Step 1 :: VE does not mean 80% are protected\
Step 2 :: VE does not apply equally to all individuals\
Step 3 :: VE depends on context and exposure

## Critical Reflection

SelfCheck\
Question :: What factors could distort vaccine effectiveness estimates?\
Answer :: Differences in exposure, reporting accuracy, and population characteristics

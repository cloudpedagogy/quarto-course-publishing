# Understanding Measures and Data in Outbreaks

In any outbreak investigation, raw numbers alone are not enough. Public health analysis depends on interpreting data relative to population size and exposure.

## What Data Is Collected?

Typical outbreak datasets include:

- number of confirmed cases

- population at risk

- vaccination status

- timing of exposure

## Key Epidemiological Measures

Definition\
Term :: Attack Rate\
Meaning :: The proportion of individuals who develop disease in a population over a defined time period

Definition\
Term :: Relative Risk\
Meaning :: A comparison of the probability of disease between two groups

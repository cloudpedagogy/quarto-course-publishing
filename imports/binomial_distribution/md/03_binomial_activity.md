# Practice Activity

## Task

A vaccination programme has an uptake rate of 0.8. In a group of 12 people, calculate the probability that exactly 10 receive the vaccine.

## Questions

1.  What values correspond to x, size, and prob?

2.  Which R function would you use?

3.  How would you interpret the result in plain language?

## Reflection

What assumptions are being made when applying the binomial distribution in this context, and when might those assumptions be unrealistic in public health practice?
